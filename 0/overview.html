Overview

What?

HTML in Hyde is a HTML Freewrite - [an event] in which people get together and write HTML documents. This one is in a park (like the Ontario based HTML in the Park)!

When?

Sunday, 21st July, 12pm (sharp!) - 3:00, 2024, 📅 calendar link

to bring?

  • a (charged) laptop
  • charger for they phone (you'll be hotspotting internet)
  • arts and crafts equipment (pencils, pens, paper, canvas, paint, etc). we're making websites, and websites need art to look cool.
  • snacky snacks
  • sunscreen and/or sunnies
  • a picnic blanket, if you have one

Where?

North-East the Anzac Memorial in Hyde Park.
🚾 Bathrooms are available in Museum Station (like a 3 min walk away).

Schedule

  • 12:00 - setup
  • 12:15 - call to action
  • 12:22 - build build build
  • 2:45 - demos
  • 3:00 - keep cooking (if you want)
